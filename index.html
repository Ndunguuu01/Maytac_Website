From Farm to Cup

Our rigorous "Fully Washed" process ensures the cleanest cup profile.

01 Selective Harvesting

Quality begins on the tree. Our partner farmers hand-pick only the deep red, ripe cherries. This selective picking ensures high sugar content and consistent flavor.

02 Wet Processing (Pulping)

Within 24 hours of harvest, cherries are pulped to remove the skin. The beans then undergo fermentation for 12-24 hours to break down the mucilage.

03 Sun Drying

The washed parchment coffee is laid out on raised African drying beds. This allows air to circulate freely. The beans are turned regularly until they reach 10.5% - 11.5% moisture.

04 Dry Milling & Export

Green beans are polished and gravity-graded by size (AA, AB, PB) and density. They are packed in GrainPro bags inside jute sacks, ready for shipment to Europe.
